What Is the VIRE Coalition?

VIRE is not a support group. It's not a venting space. It's not about safety through silence or asking the system to be nicer.

VIRE is a coalition of resilient women building the consequence layer.

We believe:

  • Harm should never be cost-free
  • Disrespect should be expensive
  • Solidarity should be public, strategic, and unbreakable

Our mission is to restore balance by making harm and entitlement obsolete, and by replacing broken systems with visible, woman-led power.

We build:

  • Tactical solidarity networks
  • High-impact culture campaigns
  • Financial and emotional infrastructure for women who no longer want to beg for dignity
