About VIRE Coalition

Our Mission & Values

The VIRE Coalition is dedicated to empowering women through solidarity and collective action. Our name reflects our core values:

Valor - We face challenges with courage and determination.

Integrity - We act according to strong moral principles.

Resilience - We adapt and recover from difficulties.

Empowerment - We help women gain confidence and strength to control their lives.

We believe that together, women can create profound change in society by standing united, just as female bonobos do in their communities.

Our Inspiration: Bonobos

Female Leadership

In bonobo societies, females hold leadership positions and work together to maintain peace and stability within their groups. This model of cooperative female leadership inspires our coalition's structure.

Coalition Building

Female bonobos form strong alliances to address threats collectively. When challenged, they respond as a united front, demonstrating that solidarity beats individual strength.

Social Harmony

Bonobos maintain peaceful societies through accountability and natural consequences for disruptive behavior. Their approach shows how communities can thrive when everyone is held accountable.

What Bonobos Do

Bonobos, our closest living relatives alongside chimpanzees, offer a remarkable model for female solidarity and conflict resolution that informs VIRE's approach:

Female Bonds

Female bonobos form strong social bonds with other females who may not be related to them. These relationships create powerful alliances that maintain social order.

Collective Response

When threatened by a male, female bonobos will rally together, regardless of their relationship status. This solidarity effectively neutralizes threats through collective action rather than individual confrontation.

Peaceful Resolution

Unlike other primates, bonobo societies resolve conflicts through alliance-building and social connections rather than aggression, resulting in more peaceful communities.
